About Keystone

Our Story, Our Promise

Our History

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

1985 - The Founding

KEYSTONE was founded with a simple vision: to provide high-quality aluminum and glass products with unparalleled craftsmanship. We started in a small workshop in Huntingdon Valley.

1998 - Expansion

Our commitment to quality led to rapid growth. We expanded our services to include specialty doors and frames, moving to our current, larger facility on Pike Rd.

2010 - Embracing Technology

We integrated advanced technology into our fabrication process, allowing for greater precision and efficiency in delivering complex glazing and metalwork projects.

Today - A Trusted Partner

Decades later, KEYSTONE stands as a leader in the industry, trusted by architects, contractors, and businesses for projects of all sizes.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted and innovative partner for architectural aluminum and glass solutions, transforming spaces through exceptional design and lasting quality.

Our Mission

To deliver superior metal and glass products through precision engineering, expert craftsmanship, and a relentless commitment to customer satisfaction, ensuring every project is completed to the highest standards of safety and excellence.
